Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (15 October 1931 â€“ 27 July 2015) was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th president of India (2002-2007).
Known as the "Missile Man of India," he contributed significantly to India's missile and nuclear programs. Supported by both major political parties, he was affectionately called the "People's President." After his presidency, Kalam focused on education and writing, receiving the Bharat Ratna.
He died from cardiac arrest on 27 July 2015 while lecturing at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong.
